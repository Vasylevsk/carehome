About us

Exertive Care Group is a specialised organisation dedicated to supporting children who come from all backgrounds. Whether due to the passing of their parents or other difficult circumstances, these young individuals deserve love, guidance, and a safe environment where they can grow, learn, and build a future.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and supportive space where every child feels valued, understood, and empowered. We recognise that children who have experienced loss or hardship need more than just basic care—they need emotional support, education, and opportunities to develop essential life skills.

That’s why we offer a holistic approach to help every child gain confidence, build relationships, and prepare for a successful future.

Our mission

Emotional and Psychological Support

Children in difficult circumstances often experience deep emotional trauma. While we do not have in-house psychologists, we are committed to helping them access the support they need. Through partnerships with professionals and community resources, we provide guidance, mentorship, and a safe space where they can express their feelings, develop emotional resilience, and regain a sense of security and hope.

Education and Skill Development

Every child deserves access to quality education and opportunities to explore their talents. We offer academic support, career guidance, vocational training, and workshops to help them develop essential skills for their future. Whether it’s preparing for exams, learning digital skills, or discovering creative passions, we ensure they have the tools to succeed.

Social Integration and Future Readiness

Helping children transition into independent adulthood is a crucial part of our work. We provide life skills training, job placement programs, financial literacy education, and social adaptation workshops. By connecting them with mentors, internships, and community initiatives, we equip them with the confidence and knowledge needed to build a stable future.

Ofsted

Exertive Care Group is approved and regulated by Ofsted.

Ofsted aims to improve lives by raising standards in education and children’s social care. They inspect and regulate thousands of organisations and individuals providing education, training and care – from childminders to training providers, schools to local authorities – and we share what we find.

Safety First
At Exertive Care Group, safeguarding is our top priority. We provide a secure and nurturing environment where children and teenagers can feel safe and supported.
Professional Care
Our dedicated team is trained to identify and address any potential risks. We work closely with professionals to ensure every child’s well-being and emotional development.
Continuous Improvement
We regularly review and update our safeguarding policies to align with the latest child protection guidelines, ensuring the highest standards of care.
A Brighter Future
By prioritising safety and emotional support, we empower young people to build their future with confidence, resilience, and hope.
