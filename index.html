About us

Love and Care in Every Moment

We believe that every child deserves love, attention, and an individualised approach. Our team of dedicated professionals creates a warm and nurturing environment where children feel safe and encouraged to express themselves. We ensure that each child feels special, valued, and surrounded by kindness and support.

Learning and Growth Through Play

Our programs go beyond basic care they focus on fostering cognitive, social, and creative development. We encourage curiosity and learning through engaging activities that make education fun. Whether through storytelling, art, or interactive play, we inspire children to explore, imagine, and discover new things every day.

A Safe and Inspiring Environment

We provide a secure, stimulating space where children can freely play, learn, and make meaningful friendships. Our care environments are filled with joy, trust, and encouragement, ensuring every family feels confident that their child is in safe and loving hands.

Ofsted

Exertive Care Group is approved and regulated by Ofsted.

Ofsted aims to improve lives by raising standards in education and children’s social care. They inspect and regulate thousands of organisations and individuals providing education, training and care – from childminders to training providers, schools to local authorities – and we share what we find.
